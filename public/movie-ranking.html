Cameron's Favorite Movies

My top 3 movies of all time

Transformers: Rise of the Beasts

Transformers: Rise of the Beasts brought a new twist to the series as it introduced a new setting and totally different character styles.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3

GOTG Vol. 3 focused more on the backstory of the characters which made it feel more personal than the other movies in the series.

The Longest Yard

The Longest Yard brought together football and comedy in a way that no other movie has in my opinion.
